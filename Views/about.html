Overview of Spring Food

We’ve been making amazing artisan cupcakes by hand, in small batches since 2018.

Springfood is a choice leading food processing and grocery store website in Bayelsa, Nigeria. Founded on 2020, we have dedicated ourselves to serve Nigerians both home and abroad (in diaspora) by providing them easy access to healthy and quality food items.
